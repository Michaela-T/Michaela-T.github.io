Ultimate Generic Clicker Game

It's a pretty simple game. kill Monsters to get Gold to buy things from the Shop, in order to kill more Monsters, etc...
there is also a Final Boss, because I thought it would be fun to make.

Click to play game

Slay monsters from the weak Hell Hound,


to the mighty Arch Demon...


Even to the Demon King himself!

Along with an Auto-Heal abillity, the Demon King has 2 special moves:
Self Heal, which recovers his health by a large amount, and...
Stun, which stuns all your companions for 5 seconds, leaving you to fight on your own.
The Demon King uses a special move every 10 seconds.
